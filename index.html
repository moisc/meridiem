The Concept

Every hour
has its ritual.

Meridiem — from the Latin for midday — is a gathering place built around the rhythm of a full day. Morning light calls for a thoughtfully sourced espresso. Evening calls for a perfectly balanced cocktail. And the hours in between deserve a space worthy of them all.
